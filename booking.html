Booking Guidelines

Securing Your Slot

To confirm your booking reservation, a non-refundable deposit of £20 is required. This processing deposit will be completely deducted from your total bill value, leaving your outstanding remaining balance to be handled cleanly in cash on arrival.

Cancellation Terms

Any alterations or cancellations to your scheduled appointments must be process-notified at least 48 hours prior to your reservation window. Cancellations executed within the 48-hour framework will result in your session deposit being strictly forfeited.

Hair Preparation

Clients are welcome to wash and dry their hair raw before attending their appointment slot. Alternatively, full clean-cleansing preparation washes can be managed seamlessly during your appointment setup for an additional service fee of £15.
